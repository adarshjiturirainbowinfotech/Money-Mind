Money Mind Privacy Policy

Transparency and trust matter to us. This policy explains what information Money Mind collects, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the app.

1. Overview

Money Mind (“the App”) is published by Adarsh Jituri (“we”, “us”, or “our”). The App helps you track expenses, receive reminders, and personalize your experience. We collect only the information necessary to offer these features, and we never sell personal data.

2. Information We Collect

Camera & Photo Library (Optional)

When you choose to capture or upload a profile photo, we request permission to access your camera or photo library. Images remain on your device unless you explicitly share them elsewhere.

Notifications & Alarms

Permissions such as notifications, exact alarms, and wake locks are used solely to deliver reminders that you schedule inside the App. You can disable these at any time from system settings.

Advertising & Analytics

Money Mind integrates Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google’s Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Information

  • Display your chosen profile image within the App.
  • Send reminders and notifications you configure.
  • Improve stability and performance through diagnostics.
  • Deliver ads and track aggregate analytics using trusted partners.

4. Sharing & Third Parties

We only share limited information with third-party services integral to the App:

  • Google AdMob — Ads delivery and reporting (may collect device identifiers).
  • Expo / React Native — Framework services required for the App’s functionality.

Each partner is contractually obligated to safeguard your data and comply with applicable laws.

5. Data Security

We apply industry-standard safeguards such as secure networking (HTTPS), least-privilege permission requests, and routine dependency updates. No security practice can guarantee absolute protection, but we actively monitor and remediate potential vulnerabilities.

6. Your Choices

  • Revoke camera or photo access via system settings; core expense features still work.
  • Disable notifications or alarms from device settings if you no longer want reminders.
  • Manage ad personalisation through your Google account or Android privacy controls.

7. Children’s Privacy

Money Mind is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. International Data Transfers

We may process data on servers located outside your country (for example, in the United States). These transfers are performed in accordance with applicable privacy regulations.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via an in-app notice and by updating the “Updated” date at the top of this page.
